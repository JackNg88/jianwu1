I am a researcher at the **Center for Infection and Genomics of the Lung (CIGL)**, Justus Liebig University Giessen, Germany. My research integrates **single-cell genomics**, **stem cell biology**, and **computational biology** to understand lung infection, chromatin dynamics, and cellular reprogramming.

I obtained my **Master of Natural Science** from the Guangzhou Institutes of Biomedicine and Health (GIBH), Chinese Academy of Sciences, under the supervision of **Prof. Duanqing Pei**, where I studied transposable elements, cancer metastasis (CTC), and chromatin accessibility during iPSC reprogramming.

I subsequently worked as a **Research Assistant** at **The University of Hong Kong (HKU)** under **Prof. Pentao Liu**, contributing to projects on expanded potential stem cells (EPSCs) in bovine, porcine and human systems, trophoblast differentiation, and decidual macrophage biology.

I am proficient in **Python**, **R**, and **Linux**, with deep expertise in bulk RNA-seq, scRNA-seq, ATAC-seq, HiC, and ChIP-seq analysis pipelines.
