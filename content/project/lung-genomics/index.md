## Overview

At the **Center for Infection and Genomics of the Lung (CIGL)**, we apply cutting-edge single-cell and spatial transcriptomics to decode the molecular landscape of lung infection and inflammation.

## Approaches

- **scRNA-seq** – profiling immune and structural lung cell populations during infection
- **Spatial transcriptomics** – mapping gene expression in tissue context
- **Multi-omics integration** – combining RNA-seq, ATAC-seq and proteomics

## Goals

Understanding host-pathogen interactions at single-cell resolution to identify novel therapeutic targets for lung diseases.
